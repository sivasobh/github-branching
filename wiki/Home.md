## Key Concepts at a Glance

**Git Flow** - Main + Develop + Feature/Release/Hotfix branches
- Best for: Large teams, multiple versions
- Complexity: Medium-High

**GitHub Flow** - Main + Feature branches
- Best for: Continuous deployment, small teams
- Complexity: Low

**Trunk-Based** - Single main branch, feature flags
- Best for: High-frequency deployment
- Complexity: Low (needs feature flags)
